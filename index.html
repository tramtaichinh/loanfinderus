FAQ - Common Questions

Is LoanFinderUS a lender or a financial advisor?

No. LoanFinderUS is not a lender, financial institution, or financial advisor. We are a free comparison service that helps you explore and compare loan options from our trusted lending partners. We do not make credit decisions or loan offers.

Will checking my loan options affect my credit score?

No. Most of our partner lenders only perform a soft credit inquiry to show you available rates. This type of check does not impact your credit score. If you proceed with a full application, a lender may perform a hard inquiry..

What is an Annual Percentage Rate (APR)?

The APR represents the total yearly cost of your loan, including the interest rate and fees (such as origination fees). It helps you understand the true cost of borrowing and compare offers more easily.

How soon can I receive funds?

Funding times vary by the lender and their verification process. Once approved, some borrowers may receive funds as soon as the next business day, depending on the lender‚Äôs policies and your bank‚Äôs processing time.

How does my credit score affect my loan options?

Your credit score helps lenders determine your rates and terms. While some lenders work with lower credit scores, higher scores often qualify you for better terms and lower rates.
