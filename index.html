Frequently Asked Questions

Can checking rates affect my credit score?
Most lenders perform a soft inquiry during initial rate checks, which typically does not impact your credit score.
Is LoanFinderUS a lender?
No. LoanFinderUS is a comparison/referral platform. We connect you to our network of providers so you can review available options.
Do I have to pay any fees to use this site?
We do not charge consumers a fee to use our comparison tools. Any lender fees, if applicable, are disclosed directly by the lender.
Can all credit profiles check rates?
Many lenders in our network may review profiles across different credit backgrounds. Eligibility still depends on each provider’s criteria.
When will I know my final loan terms?
Final terms are shown by the lender after you submit a full application. Until then, any numbers you see are estimates.
