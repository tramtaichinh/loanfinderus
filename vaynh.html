FAQ – Common Questions

Will checking my options affect my credit score?

No. Most of our partner lenders only perform a soft credit inquiry to show your available rates. This type of check does not impact your credit score. If you proceed with a full application, a lender may perform a hard inquiry.

Is LoanFinderUS a lender?

No, we’re not a lender. LoanFinderUS is an information and connection platform that helps you compare loan offers. You can review options and apply directly with trusted lenders.

How soon can I receive funds?

Funding times vary by lender and the verification process. Once approved, some borrowers may receive funds as soon as the next business day, depending on the lender’s policies.

What is an Annual Percentage Rate (APR)?

The APR represents the total yearly cost of your loan, including interest rate and fees (such as origination fees). It helps you understand the true cost of borrowing and compare offers more easily.

How does my credit score affect my loan options?

Your credit score helps lenders determine your rates and terms. While some lenders work with lower credit scores, higher scores often qualify for better terms and lower rates.

When will I get my money?

Funding times vary by lender and depend on their approval and verification process. Once a loan is approved, some lenders may release funds as soon as the next business day, depending on their policies and your financial institution’s processing time.
